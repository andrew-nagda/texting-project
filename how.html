How it Works

BrainTrain Daily sends you brief practice questions by SMS between 9:00 AM and 10:00 PM in your selected time zone. Reply with your answer to see if you’re correct.

Text Commands

  • STATS — shows answered, correct, accuracy %, and your level (Calibrating → Elite).
  • ANOTHER — get another question right away (when on-demand is enabled).
  • HINT — receive a short nudge if you’re stuck.
  • STOP — opt out anytime. START to opt back in.

Tracks

  • Consulting, Investment Banking, High Finance
  • GMAT, GRE, SAT, ACT, LSAT
  • CPA, CMA
  • CFA Level I, II, III

Why it works

  • Spaced practice — a quick daily question sticks better than marathon cramming.
  • Low friction — lives in your texts, not another app you’ll forget to open.
  • Immediate feedback — answer, check, learn.
